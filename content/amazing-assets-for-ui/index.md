<h1 id="kukla">3D Objects</h1>

Kukla is cool 3D icon library, having both a free and a paid version.

The free version has 4 objects with changeable colours, 2 scene angles and 1 character pose.

There is also a paid version, coming in at $48 (around â‚¹3,500) with 137 objects, changable colours, 2 angles, png files, 4k res, 10 character poses, and glossy + shadow icons.

Along with the previous pack, I couldn't find any info about using these on commercial projects, though it seems you can use them. Again, I advise you not to quote me on what I said, and contact the owners directly if you're using in a commercial project.

To get this pack, visit [this link](https://wannathis.one/kukla-kit)

<h1 id="slam-pack">SLAM 3D Objects Pack</h1>

These come with PNGs, along with Cinema 4D files!

You MAY NOT use this for commercial native apps, web apps or games. To read up more on this, you can check out the [license](https://craftwork.design/license/)

<h1 id="handz">3D Hands</h1>

This is amongst my favourite packs in this list as well.

This is also a 'freemium' type pack, which offers a good free demo of the pack.

The free version offers the source files (.blend) and has a total of 320 PNG files!

It includes 12 gestures with 9 skin colours, and 3 sleeve variants.

The premium version on the other hand comes in at $19 (around â‚¹1,400).

It includes everything in the free version, plus

 - 1,200+ PNG files instead of 320
 - 12 hand gestures with mobile iPhones
 - 4 hand gestures with a tablet/iPad
 - 3 hand gestures with Samsung Gear
 - 3 hand gestures with an Apple Watch
 - 2 Mac screens
 - 2 rings

...along with a tutorial on how to play around with the library.

This library mentions nothing about using in commercial projects on the page, and I couldn't find any restrictions of use on it. To stay safe, if you wish, you can contact them to make sure.

To get this pack, visit [this link](https://www.handz.design/)
